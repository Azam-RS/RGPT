## 2. Data Collection and Use

### 2.1 Why We Collect Data

At PrimisAI, we collect data to enhance your experience, provide, improve, and develop our services, understand how our services are being used, and communicate with you. Here's a closer look at the types of data we collect and the purposes for which we use them:

### 2.2 Types of Data Collected

1. **Personal Identification Information**: This includes your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other data that can identify you personally. We collect this data when you create an account, interact with our services, or communicate with us.

2. **Usage Data**: Information on how you interact with our services, such as the pages you visit on our site, your search queries, and other actions on our website.

3. **Device and Connection Information**: We gather information about the devices you use to access our services, including IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, and device settings. 

4. **Location Data**: Depending on your device settings and permissions, we may collect location data.

5. **Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies**: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your browsing activity and the way you interact with our services, to remember your preferences and settings, and to understand overall usage patterns.

### 2.3 How We Use Your Data

1. **Account Management and Service Provision**: Your personal data helps us create and manage your account, provide customer support, and deliver and improve our services.

2. **Communication**: We use your contact details to communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support messages. We also use your data for marketing purposes, in accordance with your preferences.

3. **Improvement and Personalization**: By analyzing usage data, we enhance the user experience, develop new features, and personalize our services to suit your preferences.

4. **Security and Legal Compliance**: We use your data to ensure the security of our services, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

5. **Research and Analytics**: Usage and device data help us understand trends, conduct research, and perform analytics to improve our services.

### 2.4 Consent and Control

When you provide us with personal data, you consent to our collection, use, and sharing of that information as described in this policy. You have control over your data and can update, modify, or delete your information at any time through your account settings or by contacting us directly.

## 3. User Rights and Choices

### 3.1 Your Privacy Rights

At PrimisAI, we respect your rights regarding your personal data. As a user, you are entitled to the following:

1. **Access to Your Data**: You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you.

2. **Data Correction**: If your personal data is incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct it.

3. **Data Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions dictated by legal obligations.

4. **Data Portability**: Where applicable, you have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transfer it to another data controller.

5. **Withdrawal of Consent**: For data processing based on consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

6. **Objection to Processing**: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

7. **Restriction of Processing**: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [support@primis.ai](mailto:support@primis.ai). We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

### 3.2 Managing Your Information

You can review, modify, or delete your personal data through your account settings on our website. If you require assistance, please contact our support team.

### 3.3 Opting Out of Communications

You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by using the unsubscribe link in the emails or contacting us directly.
